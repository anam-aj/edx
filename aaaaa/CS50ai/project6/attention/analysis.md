# Analysis

## Layer 8, Head 7

It appears that here adjective is paying attention to noun. Even when noun is present at different position relatively adjective still pays attention to noun. When sentence is complicated and has multiple nouns, it still works.

Example Sentences:
- a [MASK] cow is grazing.
- he is a [MASK] person.
- there is a horse in the field whose colour is [MASK].

## Layer 4, Head 6

It appeard that in this layer each token is paying attention to the preceding token. This also make sense as the word that comes next depend on the previous word.

Example Sentences:
- a [MASK] cow is grazing.
- the woman siiting there is an [MASK] one.
- he is a [MASK] person.
- food is being [MASK] by him.
- there is a horse in the field whose colour is [MASK].
